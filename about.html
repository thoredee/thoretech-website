Why I started Thoretech

I spent 25 years delivering technology to large corporations. I was good at it. But I kept noticing the same thing: the companies we worked for focused on the technology. The people actually using it were an afterthought.

What I took real pleasure in was the small stuff. Helping a team change one small thing about how they worked and watching them get time back. Those moments stuck with me.

Thoretech is built on that. Understanding what matters to you. Finding simple ways to make things better using AI and technology. And particularly with AI: it can feel daunting and expensive and out of reach. I love bridging that gap. Showing people what it can actually do for them, clearly and without the confusion.

I built this to help people, not just deliver technology.
